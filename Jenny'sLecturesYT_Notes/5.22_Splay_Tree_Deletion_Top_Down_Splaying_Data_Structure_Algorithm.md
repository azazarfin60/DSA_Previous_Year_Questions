# 5.22 Splay Tree Deletion | Top Down Splaying | Data Structure & Algorithm

---

## 📐 Top-Down Splaying
Instead of splaying bottom-up after finding the node, **top-down** splits the tree into L (smaller) and R (larger) as we search. More efficient — single pass.

## 📐 Steps
1. During search, split into three parts: **L**, **middle path**, **R**
2. When target found, assemble final tree
3. Fewer pointer changes than bottom-up

## 💡 Top-down is preferred in practice; bottom-up is conceptually simpler for exams.
