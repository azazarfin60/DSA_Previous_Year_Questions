# 7.8 Max Heap Insertion and Deletion | Heap Tree Insertion and Deletion with example | Data Structure

## 🔑 Max Heap Property: Parent ≥ Children (complete binary tree)

## 📐 Insertion (Heapify Up)
1. Insert at **end** (next available position to maintain complete tree)
2. **Heapify up**: compare with parent, swap if larger, repeat

### Example: Insert 50 into [40, 30, 20, 10, 15]
```
Tree:  40              Array index 5 = left child of index 2 (node 20)
      /  \
    30    20           Insert 50 at index 5 (under 20):
   / \
  10  15

Step 0:    40           Step 1:    40           Step 2:    50
          /  \                    /  \                    /  \
        30    20  →            30    50  →            30    40
       / \   /                / \   /                / \   /
      10 15 50              10 15 20              10 15 20

  (50 > parent 20,        (50 > parent 40,         Done!
   swap 50↔20)             swap 50↔40)
```
Array: [40,30,20,10,15] → [40,30,50,10,15,20] → **[50,30,40,10,15,20]**

## 📐 Deletion (Delete Max — Root)
1. Replace root with **last element**
2. Remove last element
3. **Heapify down**: compare with children, swap with larger child, repeat

### Example: Delete max from [50, 40, 20, 10, 15, 30]
```
Step 0:    50           Step 1:    30           Step 2:    40
          /  \                    /  \                    /  \
        40    20  →            40    20  →            30    20
       / \   /                / \                    / \
      10 15 30              10  15                 10  15

  (Replace root 50        (30 < child 40,          Done!
   with last 30)           swap 30↔40)
```

## ⏱️ Insertion: O(log n), Deletion: O(log n)
